Gow Hastings Architects Website

Web Design

— 

Gow Hastings Architects is a Toronto-based architecture and interior design studio, specializing in the design of spaces for education and the culinary arts. In an effort to increase the studio’s visibility as design experts in their field, and to strengthen their online presence, we designed a dynamic and fully responsive, content managed website to showcase the studio’s contemporary body of work and provide insight into their methodologies and thought processes. Thoughtful user interactions and an unexpected navigational and layout structure, inspired by Josef Albers’ constructivist drawings, help to create an engaging and visually memorable online experience.
